At the Airport

Today we are in the airport. After days of packing we are finally in the air port! Today we board a small plane to Atlanta, I am, so excited.When we were packing for Malawi there was a huge bag that was 75 lbs! All the bags had baseballs and spices like texjoy and ranch powder and a whole bucket of baseballs.The best part was that there was a bag as big as me,it was humongos!
